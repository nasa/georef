Our GeoRef ground application suite provides highly automated processes of calculating the lat/lon coordinates of the center point of the image as well as geo-referencing and map overlays associated with the image. Our tool also helps you create a shareable map layer that you can display in maps based on the Google Maps API and combine with other layers.

GeoRef is currently in closed beta. You must be on the trusted testers list to get access.
